About Ministry Of Health

The ministry of health was founded in 1995 by the United Nations.
Its first minister was Dr. Elizabeth Cassell.
Dr. Cassell served as minister from 1996 to 2003.This ministry has served over thousand of clinics in and around Monrovia.
During the Ebola out brake in Liberia 2014, the Ministry and it partners along with other Non Governmental Organizations, such as USAID, World Banks, among others join hands to fight the virus to keep the wellbeing of the country. The Ministry of health was the front runner in fighting the Ebola Virus.
In 2020 when the COVID-19 brake up in Liberia, the ministry of health put prevention method in to place that help regulate the spread of the virus. Some of the methods that MOH put into place were to provide public hand washing materials such as soap, Cloride, hand sanitizer etc.. The ministry also carry on awareness through the National Public Health Institute which is a sub branch of the health ministry. The MOH has her branches outside Monrovia, drugs and viccines distribution is don't by the ministry of health through one of her department called the National Public Health Institute of Liberia (NPHIL).
Each county in Liberia has branch of the ministry ie. Bong County Health Team, Lofa County Health Team, Nimba county Health Team, among others are all branches of the Ministry of health.
